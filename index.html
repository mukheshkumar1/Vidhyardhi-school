Field Trips

At Vidhyardhi School, our field trips are designed to bridge classroom learning with real-world experiences.

Trips include visits to science centers, eco-parks, historical sites, and more — guided by educators to ensure safety and learning.

Helping Hands

Our “Helping Hands” initiative teaches students empathy, social responsibility, and the value of service through community-driven programs.

Activities include food drives, environmental clean-ups, visits to shelters, and various volunteering opportunities.
